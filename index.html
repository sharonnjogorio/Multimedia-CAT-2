🦩 Save Our Flamingos

Lake Nakuru in Kenya's Rift Valley is one of the most iconic flamingo habitats in the world, a vast, shimmering shoreline turned pink by hundreds of thousands of lesser flamingos. Rising water levels, pollution and habitat disturbance now threaten their feeding grounds. This short film follows the rangers, scientists and communities working to protect them.
